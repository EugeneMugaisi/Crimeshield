Our Personell

Security is not a product,
It is a process

Pre-Employment

Our pre-employment background investigation far exceeds that of most security firms in various jurisdictions. In addition, Crime shield has employed staff of psychologists to oversee the initial screening of our personnel by writing our pre-deployment psychological inventory and interview. Furthermore, our personnel submits to a series of aptitude tests, a 5-panel drug screening and extensive in-service training in regard to criminal law and police defensive tactics. Prior to the commencement of contract with crime shield, we will provide you with the full applicant investigation of each proposed security guard.

Supervisory Personell

Crime shield provides a free of charge supervisory personnel to ensure strict adherence to rules and regulations. CS supervisors are utilized as a liaison between CS and the client. Supervisors are on duty 24 hrs a day 365 days a year for your convenience and will regularly make spot checks to prevent the issue of complacency. It is our belief that in order to assure the best quality of service that we stand by, we must keep a close relationship with our personnel both on and off duty

Communication

At CS all our security guards will be equipped with two-way radios or an equivalent to ensure constant communication with our management team. CS also has equipped the guards with our unique software in their kit that allows for spot-on GPS enabled device that our clients can also know the constant patrols being made by the guards, this is to ensure sufficient monitoring to the satisfaction of both the client and CS team and curb complacency at work.

Servicing

Each and every security guard will be serviced regularly regarding your specific site to further provide the protection we provide. CS has taken a unique approach to the business of contract security, many of our competitors employ minimally screened and trained guards. Our approach involves a theory of well-groomed exceptionally trained uniformed security guards. Our strategy entails several steps to mitigate the possibility and opportunity of theft of property or injury to persons within the facility. We also merge the medical realm by having an updated database of our medical team who are on standby in case there is a medical emergency and will offer first aid thus saving lives.
