About Us

CRIMESHIELD SECURITY LIMITED

Crimeshield Security Limited is a 100% ex-military-owned security company founded by an experienced former Military police and HAZMAT Technician. Located in Nairobi and Western Regions at its launch, the business provides security risk analysis, security vulnerability assessment, active shooter incidents, crime prevention through environmental design, perimeter protection, security guards, security audits, and referrals to equipment providers to commercial buildings, retail businesses, and, eventually special event venues and other clients. The business is seeking to expand its clientele. Crimeshield grows its employee base onsecurity practitioners based on client contracts; both full-time and part-time personnel. The current political climate, with increased fears of terrorist activity together with the current economic reality has rendered Kenya a target. Crimeshield Security Limited was founded to mitigate the risks to as low as reasonably practicable. The company targets public, private institutions, cooperate companies, large retail stores, building management companies as well as individuals.

Our Personell

Pre-Employment

Our pre-employment background investigation far exceeds that of most security firms in various jurisdictions. In addition, Crimeshield has employed staff of psychologists to oversee the initial screening of our personnel by writing our pre-deployment psychological inventory and interview. Furthermore, our personnel submits to a series of aptitude tests, a 5-panel drug screening and extensive in-service training in regard to criminal law and police defensive tactics. Prior to the commencement of contract with Crimeshield, we will provide you with the full applicant investigation of each proposed security guard.

Supervisory Personell

Crimeshield provides a free of charge supervisory personnel to ensure strict adherence to rules and regulations. CSSL supervisors are utilized as a liaison between CSSL and the client. Supervisors are on duty 24 hrs a day 365 days a year for your convenience and will regularly make spot checks to prevent the issue of complacency. It is our belief that in order to assure the best quality of service that we stand by, we must keep a close relationship with our personnel both on and off duty

Communication

At CSSL all our security guards will be equipped with two-way radios or an equivalent to ensure constant communication with our management team. CSSL also has equipped the guards with our unique software in their kit that allows for spot-on GPS-enabled device that our clients can also know the constant patrols being made by the guards, this is to ensure sufficient monitoring to the satisfaction of both the client and CSSL team and curb complacency at work.

Servicing

Each and every security guard will be serviced regularly regarding your specific site to further provide the protection we provide. CSSL has taken a unique approach to the business of contract security. Many of our competitors employ minimally screened and trained guards. Our approach involves a theory of well-groomed, exceptionally-trained, uniformed security guards. Our strategy entails several steps to mitigate the possibility and opportunity of theft of property or injury to persons within the facility. We also merge the medical realm by having an updated database of our medical team who are on standby in case there is a medical emergency and will offer first aid thus saving lives.
